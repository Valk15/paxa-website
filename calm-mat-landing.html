The method

The protocol in three steps.

01
Prepare

Spread a high-value food (xylitol-free peanut butter, plain yoghurt, or wet food) across the mat 5 minutes before departure. This is the only time your dog receives this food.

02
Condition

Present the mat as you leave — not before, not after. The timing is the conditioning. Your dog's amygdala begins to associate your departure with the most rewarding experience of the day.

03
Rotate

Alternate between the two mats each day. Novelty resets the neurological engagement response and sustains parasympathetic activation past the 40-minute cortisol peak.
